Australian Climate Explorer

This site shows the monthly rainfall and temperature (min/max) for around 1,500 weather stations across Australia, averaged over the past 20 years.

Click on a weather station to see its historical data records.

The source of the data is the Australian Bureau of Meteorology's Climate Data Online page. I wrote a script to download the records for each station and compiled the results into a file geodatabase. I'm presuming this is legal since it's possible to access each site's data manually...
